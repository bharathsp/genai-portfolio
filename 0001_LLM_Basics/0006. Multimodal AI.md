# 🎨🧠 Multimodal AI in LLMs

---

## 🧩 1. What is Multimodal AI? (Simple Definition)

**Multimodal AI** refers to AI systems that can **understand and work with multiple types of data (modalities)** at the same time.

👉 These modalities include:

* 📝 Text
* 🖼️ Images
* 🎧 Audio
* 🎥 Video

👉 In short:
**Multimodal AI = AI that can see 👀, hear 👂, and read 📖**

---

## 💡 2. Explanation in Simple Terms

Traditional LLMs:

* Work only with **text**

Multimodal LLMs:

* Can take **different inputs together**
* Understand relationships between them

---

### 🔍 Example

Input:

* Image of a dog 🐶
* Text: “What is this animal doing?”

👉 Multimodal AI:

* Looks at image
* Understands context
* Answers: “The dog is running on grass”

---

👉 It combines:

* Visual understanding 🖼️
* Language understanding 📝
